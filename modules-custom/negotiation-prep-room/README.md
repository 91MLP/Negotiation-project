# Negotiation Prep Room

> AI-powered negotiation preparation — salary, freelance rate, car purchase, lease, and more.

## What it does

Enter the context of any negotiation. AI generates your complete prep kit in seconds:

- **BATNA assessment** — how strong is your walk-away position
- **Anchor point** — the specific number to open with and why
- **3 likely counteroffers** — with word-for-word scripts for each
- **Closing script** — how to seal the deal once you're close
- **Red flags** — tactics to watch out for

Set an **aggressiveness slider** (Collaborative → Hardball) to calibrate the tone of every script to your style.

After the negotiation, log your outcome (won / partial / lost / no-deal) and track your win rate over time.

## Features

- Salary, freelance rate, car, lease, or custom negotiation types
- AI generation via Claude (`claude-sonnet-4-6`)
- Aggressiveness slider (1–5) baked into every script
- Dashboard with win rate % and total sessions
- Full history with expandable prep kits and outcome logging

## Stack

Built as an ARI module — Next.js 16, React 19, Drizzle ORM, TanStack Query, Better Auth, Tailwind + shadcn/ui.
